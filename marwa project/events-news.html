Open Day welcomes high school students to explore campus

The university recently hosted an Open Day event to welcome high school students from across the region

  • Activities included: Campus tours, interactive sessions, and student showcases
  • Goal: Help future students experience university life and explore academic programs
  • Guided by: Faculty members and current university students

The event was a great opportunity for students to ask questions, meet professors, and get a feel for the campus environment.

More events like this are planned in the coming semesters!
