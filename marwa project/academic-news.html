Computer Science Department offers new AI course next semester

The Computer Science Department has announced a new elective course in Artificial Intelligence (AI) starting next semester.

  • Course Title: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
  • Focus Areas: Machine learning, neural networks, and real-world AI applications
  • Available for: 3rd and 4th year CS students

This course is part of the department's effort to keep up with the growing demand for AI skills in the job market.

Interested students can register during the upcoming course selection period
